# The Aster Codex

These are the writings the app rests on. Not documentation of features —
the philosophical ground beneath the work. They are shared here for
anyone curious about *why* Aster takes the posture it does.

Two books are included here.

## [Book I — The Heart of Aster](./Book%20I%20%E2%80%94%20The%20Heart%20of%20Aster.md)

The philosophical foundation. What wisdom is, why it cannot be given,
and why Aster is built as a companion rather than an authority. Sets
out the posture beneath everything else: that the work of understanding
belongs to the individual, and that a companion only helps make space
for it.

If you want to know what Aster *is for*, start here.

## [Book V — The Journey of Becoming](./Book%20V%20%E2%80%94%20The%20Journey%20of%20Becoming.md)

The lived version. Twelve chapters on conditions every person meets —
stillness, fear, resistance, honesty, courage, suffering, identity,
self-trust, change, love, meaning, flourishing — each paired with a
conversation showing what participation actually looks like. The voice
exemplars the app's writing is calibrated against are drawn from this
book.

If you want to know what *talking with Aster feels like*, read a chapter.

---

Each book is included as `.docx` (canonical) and `.md` (renders inline
on GitHub). Three other books exist in the Codex (II — *The
Constitution of Aster*, III — *The Inner Vows of Aster*, IV — *The
Companion's Manual*) but are not part of this public release.
